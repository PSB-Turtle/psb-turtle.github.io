Sea Turtle Ecology
& Population Dynamics

The Sea Turtle Ecology and Population Dynamics group at the NEFSC is tasked with collecting information from sea turtles, analyzing data, and building statistical and simulation models to better understand the ecology of sea turtles in the Greater Atlantic region.
